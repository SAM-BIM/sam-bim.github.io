SAM — Sustainable Analytical Model

Open-source analytical modelling toolkit for building energy and environmental design. Canonical upstream: github.com/SAM-BIM/SAM

Core concepts

Interoperability

Push/pull with Revit and Rhino/Grasshopper. Exchange via IFC, gbXML, Excel. Connect to Honeybee, Tas, Topologic, BHoM and more.

Open & Extensible

LGPL-3.0-or-later licensed core. Clean APIs and modular architecture. Contributions welcome via GitHub.

Validated

Unit tests and CI. Example datasets referencing PsychroLib 2.5.0 and published benchmarks.
